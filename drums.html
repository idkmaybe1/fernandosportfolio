My Drumming Journey

I started playing the drums in 6th grade when my music teacher introduced me to the djembe, a drum similar to the bongo. I learned how to play several songs and even bought my own! In 7th grade my dad bought me a drum set which I fell in love with. However, my neighbors complained, which put a temporary end to this hobby. However, my passion for drumming never vanished. When listening to music, I would often envision myself the drums to that song. Recently, we moved, so I am finally able to play the drums again! I have not recorded much drum content as I write this, but when I do, I will definitely add videos here!
