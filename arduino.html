My Arduino Journey

I first used Arduino in my school's robotics club. I learned how to wire and program LEDs using the Arduino IDE. In the club, I built a car that is controlled with a PS2 Controller. I also became familiar with many sensors, motors, and other electronics. When the summer started, I purchased my own electronics kit and dedicated a lot of time to building gadgets and devices.
